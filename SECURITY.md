# Security & Privacy

Timo stores your city pairs on-device, doesn't require an account, and syncs only through your own iCloud account. Location is processed on-device through Apple's frameworks and never sent to us. See the full [Privacy Policy](https://trytimo.com/privacy) for details on what data the app touches and how it's handled.

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you've found a security issue in Timo, please don't open a public issue for it. Instead, email **timo@loam.sk** with details and, if possible, steps to reproduce. Reports are acknowledged as soon as possible.
